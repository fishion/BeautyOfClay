Workshops

The Workshops I run are suitable for all ages, including children, and there are a variety of different types or workshops to suit all needs and budgets, including:

  • Regular Public Community Pottery Workshops in community spaces. These are great for families working together or as a taster session.
  • Themed Private Workshop Series where over the course of a few sessions I will help you design and create your own pieces to take home.
  • I can also arrange to do bespoke and individual sessions with you in your home at very reasonable prices. This would be suitable for e.g. birthday parties, business events, groups of friends, or if you would like 1-2-1 guidance in a design, or if you have a specific project you would like to work on. Please contact me to let me know what you would like.

In all cases, no experience is necessary and all materials are provided. Examples of some previous workshops as well as some customer testamonials are also below
