Workshops

Workshop Information

  • Workshops are suitable for all ages, including children
  • Bespoke and individual sessions with you in your home
  • Workshops for family groups with adults and children working together
  • Workshops for birthday parties, business events, groups of friends.
  • 1-2-1 guiding in any design for your home if you have a specific project to work on.
  • No experience is necessary and all materials are provided.

Ceramic Coffee Table Workshops
In this five week course you will learn the key elements of Persian Ceramic design and how to create & draw with glaze. At the end of the course you will make your own unique ceramic coffee table. The cost for wooden table will included in the course fee.
Duration: 5 weeks
Fee: £210
Location: 28 Chichester Close, BN3 8ET, Hove

Duration: 2 weeks
Fee: £60
